Real Power.

Through Participatory Budgeting, community members - like you - directly decide how to spend at least $250k of the public budget.

If you have an idea about how things could work better in your community, share it on the map. Check out the guidelines to see what ideas can be funded.

Community members will then review your idea, alongside all the others, based on its feasibility, the need it would address, and the number of people it would benefit.

The top projects will be put on a ballot, and next Spring, all residents ages 14 and older in participating districts will vote on their top projects.

For more information on participatory budgeting in Long Beach, visit www.pblongbeach.org. For information on the nationwide movement for PB, visit the website of the Participatory Budgeting Project.
