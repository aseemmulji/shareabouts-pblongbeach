{{#_}}Guidelines{{/_}}

The idea submission period will re-open this fall, when you’ll be able to propose your ideas here or at a local assembly. Eligible ideas must be for "capital" projects: physical infrastructure for public benefit, such as park improvements or new technology for schools. “Expense" projects, such as afterschool programs or expanding bus service, are not eligible.

In the meantime, you can view this past year’s winning projects and browse, support, and comment on other ideas that were proposed online.
